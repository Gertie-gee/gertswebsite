I am an artist based in Magandjin/Brisbane.

Drawing on pop culture influences and connection to Country, I create textiles, wearable works, and murals.

A proud Aboriginal woman with cultural connections to Kaurna, Barkindji, and Dja Dja Wurrung Countries, my practice explores continuity and reconnection to culture.

Commemorative matching t-shirts are my love language, and I specialise in making them for family and friends.
